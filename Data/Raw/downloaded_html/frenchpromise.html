Rediscovering Nmes

Last week, I was invited by theNmes Tourist Officeto see what the city now has to offer. I came awayenchanted and inspired. Not only is the city just as beautiful as I remembered, but it has also undergone a quiet transformation into a destination worthy of a luxury stay. There are nowthree five-star hotels, two of which are right in the heart of the old town. And of course, the Roman landmarks are still as impressive as everthe Arena,the Maison Carre(now aUNESCO World Heritage Site), and aspectacular museum that traces the citys Roman history through artifacts and statuesall excavated in Nmes itself.
