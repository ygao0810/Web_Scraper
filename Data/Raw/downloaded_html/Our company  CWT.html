About CWT

A Business-to-Business-for-Employees (B2B4E) travel management platform

CONTACT US

At CWT we focus on B2B4E, short for "business to business for employees." We make it our mission to bring the best customer experience to you and your employees.

With our long history in travel we understand travelers and are continuously innovating our processes and tools to improve business travelers' experiences.

CWT Meetings & Events (M&E) is our award-winning global corporate meetings & events management service. CWT M&E delivers comprehensive live, virtual and hybrid program solutions for thousands of customers every year.  

We hold ourselves and everyone we work with to the highest standards of ethics and conduct. Our Environmental, Social and Governance (ESG) approach resulted in 3 gold and 2 platinum EcoVadis awards for 5 consecutive years.

How we act to be socially and environmentally responsible

Environmental, Social and Governance (ESG) at CWT

CWT is deeply committed to sustainability/corporate social responsibility (CSR), which we call Environmental, Social and Governance (ESG). It is important to us that our strategy focuses on the areas of most importance to CWT and our stakeholders. To understand their views, we engage with our employees, customers and suppliers on a regular basis and consider their feedback when deciding on the issues and opportunities to address and report on.
