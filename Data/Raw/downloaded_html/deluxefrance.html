about us

We always go the extra mile for you!

- Deluxe France is a premier DMC that specializes in luxury travel experiences across France tailored for individual travelers.

- We have established long-lasting cooperation with the travel industry and are proud to be part of the preferred suppliers of leading U.S travel agencies networks such as Virtuoso, and Internova.

- In addition, we actively engage with the industry through participation in events such as the DUCO France trade show. For over 15 years, we've been proud members of the esteemed Lush Experiences collection. This enduring partnerships has been instrumental in cultivating strong B2B relationships with travel agents across North America.

- One of our key distinguishing features as a DMC is to originate from a chauffeur-driven service company, allowing us to provide you or your clients with our own exclusive fleet of luxury vehicles. A large majority of our drivers, driver-guides, and tour guides are directly employed by us, enabling us to maintain optimal flexibility and take proactive measures in last-minute situations.

- There is no minimum amount to work with us. Whether it's a VIP meet-and-greet service, airport transfers, or crafting a comprehensive itinerary from start to finish including luxury accommodations, we cater to all needs.

- Our quotations are always transparent, providing detailed breakdowns rates.

- We have built this website to serve as a valuable tool for travel advisors, offering assistance during the initial stages of the booking process.
