Meet the CEO

Hi, Im Brittany!

Im an LA based fashionista with a love for all the finer things in life from luxury fashion, fine dining, and of course international travel. Im obsessed with experiencing the best of the best each country has to offer and this has lead me to solo travel all across the globe to places including:

Dubai

Italy

France

London

Scotland

Greece

And More

I know theres a ton of tour companies and group trips out there but none of them had itineraries or activities centered around my interests of fashion and high-end experiences. So I went at it on my own.

Listen, Im a girl with a taste for the finer things in life so I had to make sure everything from accommodations to the restaurants were up to my standards and if youre still reading this youre probably the same way.

Im into getting dressed up and spa days, not hikingoranything outdoors related for that matter.

Anyways, I posted my travels all over socials and got so many questions about how I put together my trip. So I thought why not invited a group of like-minded girlies who share the same passions and interests.

These group trips are specially curated to give you the VIP experience of best these destinations have to offer while immersing you in the culture and of course getting all your content in.

I mean did you even go to Paris without getting the perfect Eiffel Tower pic?

Or London without that red Photo Booth? Ofc not!

Planning your dream trip can be stressful. But it doesnt have to be. With Luxe Fashion Trips weve planned out the perfect itinerary for an unforgettable trip, you dont have to lift a finger.

All you have to do is book that flight and show up. So buckle up and lets embark on this journey together, the luxe way.
